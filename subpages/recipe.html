A pizza

Prep.time: 30min

Cooking: 1hr

Additional: 20min

Servings: 8

Cost: 30$

Calories 521kcal

Save
Print
Share
Pin

Posted by: Anna Reed

Verified author

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 can of tomato sauce
  • 5 basil leaves

    • Need to cook for more?

    Original recipe yields 4 portions.

Preparation

  1. Take the dough you have prepared previously and form it.
  2. Spread the sauce on the flattened dough.
  3. Cut the toppings and put them on top of the sauce.
  4. Bake the pizza in 220 degrees for 20 minutes.
  5. Serve with fresh basil on top.
